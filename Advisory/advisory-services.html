Advisory Services

Most projects carry more uncertainty than is visible at the start. Operational gaps may not have been examined, data may not have been collected, and problems can emerge later when they are harder to fix. This is the upstream work that brings these issues forward early: due diligence across water, wastewater, and resource recovery that gives every downstream decision a solid foundation.

What we cover

Flexible by design. We start where uncertainty is highest — upstream due diligence, feasibility checks, and the engagement inputs that allow solution providers to price and verify performance with clarity.

How Advisory Services connect to the broader engagement

Advisory Services

Clarify outcomes, constraints, and decision logic before options are compared. The work that sets the scope before planning begins.

Systems Planning (FlowPlan)

Map the full picture across water, reuse, residuals, and energy, so every stream has a defined route, connections and constraints are confirmed before design begins, and the scope vendors receive reflects decisions that have been tested, not assumed.

Specialist support

Design and sourcing advisory for the decisions that follow planning: equipment selection and technology fit, sourcing strategy, integration and automation requirements, and implementation support through to commissioning. The work that turns a confirmed scope into a deliverable scheme.

EnWater Design

Independent water, wastewater treatment and reuse consulting, helping owners and operators turn complex challenges into clear decisions and practical outcomes.
